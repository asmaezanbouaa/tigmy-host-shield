INTERNAL RULES — SHORT-TERM RENTAL

1. General conduct
Guests agree to respect the property, its equipment, and the neighborhood. Any party, loud gathering, or unauthorized event is strictly forbidden.

2. Prohibited activities
The following are strictly forbidden: unauthorized parties, any illegal activity, drug use or trafficking, prostitution, intentional or negligent damage to the property, disturbing neighbors (excessive noise, loud music, shouting), and any behavior that endangers people or the property.

3. Occupancy
The property may only be occupied by persons declared during registration. Any additional undeclared guest requires prior authorization.

4. Quiet hours
Between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM, quiet must be maintained so as not to disturb neighbors.

5. Safety
Lock doors and windows when away. Do not smoke indoors unless otherwise indicated. Report any incident or damage to the owner or administrator immediately.

6. Sanctions
In case of violation of these rules, the owner reserves the right to terminate the stay without refund and to pursue civil or criminal action in accordance with applicable law.

7. Personal data
Information collected is used solely for the legal registration of travelers and rental management.

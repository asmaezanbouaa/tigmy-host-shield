Host guide

Quick reference for {{ app_name }} — share the guest link, review registrations, and keep records for authorities.

1. Share the guest registration link

Send guests this link before they arrive (Airbnb message, WhatsApp, or QR code in the apartment):

{{ guest_register_url }}

Or open Share with guests to copy the link or download a QR code.

2. Sign in to the admin dashboard

Bookmark this page for you and your co-host:

{{ admin_login_url }}

Use your username and password. Change your password anytime under Profile.

3. When a guest submits

  1. Open Submissions or check the Dashboard for “Needs action”.
  2. Open the guest record and review their details and ID scan.
  3. Click Verify ID once you have checked the document matches the form.
  4. Optionally run AI check if enabled (local OCR or API).
  5. Click Confirm when everything is correct, or mark Issue if something is wrong.

ID scans are automatically deleted {{ id_retention_hours | default(14) }} hours after you verify them (data protection).

4. Download PDFs for authorities

From a submission detail page you can download:

  • Fiche de police — official guest record (French + English pages)
  • Signed rules — rental rules the guest accepted

These PDFs include Morocco header styling and are generated for each registration.

5. Calendar and archive

  • Calendar — see who arrives and departs each month.
  • Archive — move old records out of the active list; export quarterly ZIPs for your records.
  • Export CSV — from Submissions, filter and export a spreadsheet.

Daily routine (2 minutes)

  1. Check Dashboard for arrivals today and pending ID verifications.
  2. Verify any new submissions before check-in.
  3. Confirm guests once ID and dates look correct.
